FAQ

Is A-Sense an AI chatbot advertising platform or GPT monetization solution?

Both. A-Sense is an AI-native ad network designed for conversational UIs to monetize chatbot and GPT Store traffic.

How do you prevent fraudulent clicks?

We block bot user agents, cap repeat clicks from the same IP, and filter speed clicks immediately after impressions to protect spend.

How does settlement work?

Settlement follows a minimum threshold policy and transparent revenue share. You can verify status and payouts in the dashboard.
